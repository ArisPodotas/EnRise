About us


EnRise is an emerging biotech startup that leverages scientific knowledge to enhance human life, environmental sustainability, and economic growth. We are currently focused on creating sustainable alternatives to traditional paper products. Our flagship product, biopaper, is the world's first paper made entirely through microbial fermentation of industrial waste, eliminating the need to cut down any trees.

Scientific background


The ability of bacteria to produce cellulose has been known since 1886, when A. J. Brown discovered it. Despite being the subject of academic research, it has had limited industrial applications, mainly as a food additive (nata de coco). Since cellulose is the primary material extracted from wood for paper pulp, we thought of using bacterial cellulose for paper production. George, (founder of EnRise) who originally came up with this idea, set up a small lab in his living room and began experimenting. After three months, he successfully made his first bio-paper.
